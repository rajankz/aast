Chinese Cultural Event


The Chinese Student and Scholar Associations of the D.C. metropolitan area are holding their Chinese Culture Festival this Sunday, Sept. 25.

All university students, faculty members, and their families and friends are welcome.
You do not need to be Chinese or speak Chinese to attend this event. Admission is free. There will be games, prizes, and a raffle!

Special guests include: President Wallace Loh, Officials from the Chinese Embassy and business sponsors.

Festival Schedule:
11:00am - 5:00pm - Exhibition @ Grand Ballroom, Stamp Student Union, University of Maryland - College Park
6:30pm - 9:00pm - Performance @ Dekelboum Concert Hall, Clarice Smith Performing Arts Center, University of Maryland - College Park

The exhibition will showcase Chinese tea culture, traditional clothing, calligraphy, folk music and instruments, sports, Kung Fu, garden landscaping, Confucian philosophy, etc. Desserts and souvenirs will be for sale at the event.
The night performance will showcase dances, songs, Kung Fu, a magic show, and a fashion show.

Pre-registration to the Chinese Cultural Festival is required.
For UMD students, faculty and staff, please register here.
For people who are interested in this event outside of UMD, please register here.
Pre-registration is valid through Sept. 21 at 11:59pm. After your registration, there will be a confirmation email sent to you. Parking is available next to the venue. Please follow standard UMD parking lot assignments.
