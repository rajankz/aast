Alumni

The Asian American Studies Program is very proud of the accomplishments of our distinguished graduates. This program would not have been started without the help of students, many of whom have gone on to continue supporting us as alumni.


For more information on the University of Maryland Alumni Association, please visit their website. If you would like to lend your support to the Asian American Studies Program, housed under the Office of Undergraduate Studies, please visit the Donations Page.


Thanks for your continuing support, which guarantees that future students can get their education at the University of Maryland!
