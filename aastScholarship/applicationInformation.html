Application Procedures

The following are procedures for scholarship applications:

  1. Download : 2012-2013 AAST Scholarship Application
  2. Complete the Scholarship Application Packet (incomplete applications will not be reviewed).
  3. Application packets can be e-mailed to aast@umd.edu or mailed/hand-delivered to the address below:
    Attn: AAST Scholarship Committee
    Asian American Studies Program
    University of Maryland
    1145 Cole Student Activities Building
    College Park, MD 20742
  4. Submit complete Application Packet by 5:00 pm on Friday, March 30, 2012.
  5. Recipients will be notified in mid-April 2012.
